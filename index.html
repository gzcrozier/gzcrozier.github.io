Pam Crozier

County Councilor At-Large

As a member of the Jefferson County Council I pledge to:

  • Focus on promoting our local economy
  • Ensure that our first responders have all the resources they need
  • Be a responsible steward of our hard-earned tax dollars

By never losing sight of these things, as well as my passion for our community, I aim to help Jefferson County become an even better, safer, and freer place to live and raise a family.
